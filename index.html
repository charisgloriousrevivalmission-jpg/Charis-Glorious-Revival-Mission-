Who we are

About Charis Glorious Revival Mission

Charis Glorious Revival Mission is a Bible-believing church family based in Ikorodu, Lagos. Our name carries our heart: charis — the grace of God — poured out to revive hearts and prepare a people for the soon return of Christ.

We are, simply, the family that cares — for the soul still searching, the believer growing in the Word, the family under pressure, and the neighbour outside our doors. Every service, ministry, and outreach we run flows from this one desire: to see lives wholly transformed by the undiluted gospel of salvation.

Why we exist

Our Vision & Mission

To raise a people prepared for the coming of Christ

We are building a body of believers walking in truth, holiness, and love — watching, ready, and undistracted, like servants who know their Master is coming soon.

“And ye yourselves like unto men that wait for their lord, when he will return from the wedding; that when he cometh and knocketh, they may open unto him immediately.” Luke 12:36

To preach the undiluted gospel of salvation

We disciple believers into maturity, revive the spirit of love in the body of Christ, and prepare souls for eternity with Christ — holding nothing back from the whole counsel of God's word.

“Go ye therefore, and teach all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father, and of the Son, and of the Holy Ghost: teaching them to observe all things whatsoever I have commanded you.” Matthew 28:19–20
